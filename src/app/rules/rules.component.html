How to play Woohoojin Bridges

Woohoojin Bridges is a web implementation of Hashiwokakero.


Rules

The goal is to connect all of the islands by drawing a series of bridges between the islands.
1. Bridges must not cross any other bridges or islands.
2. The number of bridges connected to each island must match the number on that island.
3. The bridges must connect the islands into a single connected group.

Controls

Bridges can be built using a few different methods.
1. Place your mouse over an island and press 'wasd/WASD'.
- Lowercase 'wasd' will build one bridge.
- Uppercase 'WASD' will build two bridges.
2. Click and drag your mouse from one island towards another island.
- A left click will build one bridge.
- A right click will build two bridges.
3. Shift click on an island to build bridges in all possible directions.
